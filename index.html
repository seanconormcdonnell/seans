About

Sean McDonnell is a design-oriented front-end developer. He strives to build immersive and beautiful web experiences through carefully crafted code and user-centric design.

It all started when Sean had to take a C++ class to get some IT certifications for a tech support job he had. He immediatly fell in love with code. He took several coding classes and quickly landed a front-end job at Fellowship Church. There he got to work on a wide variety of projects, with a great team, and grow a lot as a developer.

In late 2016 Sean headed for new opportunities at Brushfire. Working remotly at Brushfire was both challenging and fun. Sean got the chance to stretch his legs as a designer and collaborate with some very tallented individuals.

Expertise

Sean has a strong background working with HTML, CSS, SASS, LESS, JS, and JQUERY. He has spent a lot of time working with PhoneGap/Cordova, tvOS, ASP.Net MVC and React. He has worked extensivly with popular development tools such as GIT, GULP, Trello, PhotoShop, Illustrator and Sketch. Most importantly he has experience learning and problem solving both as an individual and as part of a team.
